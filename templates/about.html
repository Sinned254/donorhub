About Charity Connect

Our mission is to bridge the gap between generous angels and institutions in need. Whether you have clothing, food, furniture, or other items to share, or you wish to offer services, Charity Connect provides a platform to easily connect with organizations making a difference in your community.

We aim to make the process of giving as simple and impactful as possible, ensuring that your gifts reach the organizations that need them most. We believe in the power of community and the positive change that can happen when those who have are connected with those who need.
